Sitemaps and Feeds

Sitemaps

If you have a large site, you may want to have a sitemap.

To enable sitemaps, head into your Site Settings and add a new list metadata called gems, with an entry called jekyll-sitemap. Save, publish, and you’re done! This will automatically create a /sitemap.xml for you.

For more info, view the jekyll-sitemap docs.

Feeds

Adding an Atom or RSS feed is similar to adding a sitemap.

Head into your Site Settings and add a new list metadata called gems, with an entry called jekyll-feed. Save, publish, and you’re done! This will automatically create a feed.xml file for you.

For more info, view the jekyll-feed docs.
